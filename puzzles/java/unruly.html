Unruly

from Simon Tatham's Portable Puzzle Collection

Colour every square either black or white, in such a way that:

  • no three consecutive squares, horizontally or vertically, are the same colour
  • each row and column contains the same number of black and white squares.

Left-click in an empty square to turn it black it out, or right-click to turn it white. Click again in an already-filled square to cycle it between black and white and empty; middle-click to reset any square to empty.
