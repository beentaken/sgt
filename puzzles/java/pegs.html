Pegs

from Simon Tatham's Portable Puzzle Collection

Jump one peg over another to remove the one you jumped over. Try to remove all but one peg.

Drag a peg into an empty space to make a move. The target space must be exactly two holes away from the starting peg, in an orthogonal direction, and there must be a peg in the hole in between.
